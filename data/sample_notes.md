\# Demo Notes



RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation.



This app can upload notes, extract text using OCR, store chunks in ChromaDB, and answer questions using a local Ollama model.



ChromaDB is used as the vector database.



LlamaIndex is used to load, chunk, index, and retrieve notes.



Tesseract OCR is used to extract text from images and scanned PDFs.
